Ocean Raised,
Life Tested

I've always been a water person. In Sweden, when I was a baby, my parents carried a little tub everywhere so I could sit in water to calm down and play. At two, I was on a windsurf board with my dad.

I've lived across the world — Sri Lanka, Portugal, Guatemala, El Salvador, Mexico — chasing waves, community, and freedom. Surfing grounded me, humbled me, pushed me, and rebuilt me. It gave me direction when life hit hard and peace when nothing else made sense.

I've been coaching for years across different cultures and breaks. My approach is simple: real technique, real ocean understanding, real presence, patience, awareness, honesty, and respect for the lineup.

I coach instinctively. I read people as much as I read the water. Some surfers need calm and safety. Some need structure. Some need to be challenged. Some just need someone who believes in them while they remember who they are.

SALTYREBEL is a place for people who love the ocean and want to live fully. With presence, strength, fun, and real connection. Come as you are. Leave with salt in your hair, more confidence in your body, and more ocean in your life.

Private Coaching

My coaching is personal, precise, and deeply attentive. No chaos. No ego. No shouting from the shore. I'm in the water with you, noticing everything, guiding with clarity and calm.

What You'll Learn: Ocean intelligence, lineup awareness, technical foundations, flow & decision-making, emotional calm, true independence, wave selection.

Formats: 1:1 private coaching, 2-3 person semi-private, small group (max 4)

I coach in English & Swedish, and I can guide in Spanish when needed.

Retreats

We surf hard. We rest well. We enjoy life. Sometimes we go to bed early, sometimes we dance with sand on our feet. No pressure — just flow and vibe.

You wake up by the ocean. The kind of morning where your body already feels softer and stronger at the same time. Coffee in your hand, sun on your face, and that feeling like the world finally slowed down enough for you to breathe again.

You surf, you learn, you laugh when you fall, you surprise yourself when you don't. Technique clicks. Confidence grows. Your body remembers strength. Your mind remembers calm.

By the end, your surfing leveled up. Your body feels alive. Your head feels clear. You feel proud, lighter, connected and like you gained friends you don't have to explain yourself to.
